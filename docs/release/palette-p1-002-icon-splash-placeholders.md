## Design Language

Assets match the app's diegetic aesthetic:

- **Background** `#050608` — deepspace black (same as `app.json backgroundColor`).
- **Primary accent** `#00FFCC` — CyberTrader cyan; used for hex ring, circuit nodes, CT mark, and splash title.
- **Secondary accent** `#00997A` — muted cyan; used for inner circuit ring and sub-title.
- **Hex structure** — concentric dashed hexagonal circuit rings with vertex nodes; `CT` monogram centred.
- **Splash** — portrait badge + title block with scan-line texture; `CYBERTRADER / AGE OF PANTHEON` typeset in Courier New Bold.
