# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: April 29, 2026*

**CyberTrader: Age of Pantheon** ("the Game") is a simulated trading experience that does **not** involve real money, real financial instruments, or real cryptocurrency transactions. All in‑game currency is fictional and has no monetary value.

### Data Collected
- **Local gameplay data** stored on the device (player progress, inventory, settings). This data never leaves the device unless the player opts‑in to sync via SupabaseAuthority (currently disabled behind a feature flag).
- **Analytics** (optional) – anonymous usage statistics may be collected via Expo's `expo-firebase-analytics` if the player enables it in Settings. No personally‑identifiable information (PII) is sent.
- **Crash reports** – error stack traces are captured locally and sent anonymously to help improve stability. No device identifiers are included.

### What We Do Not Collect
- Real‑world financial information.
- Personal identifiers such as name, email, phone number, or payment details.
- Location data beyond optional anonymous aggregate analytics.

### Your Choices
- You can delete all local data by selecting **Reset Game** in Settings.
- You may opt‑out of analytics and crash reporting at any time in Settings.
- If you enable SupabaseAuthority, you will be prompted to create a local handle; no external authentication is required.

### Children’s Privacy
The Game is intended for players aged **13+**. No personal data from children under 13 is collected.

### Contact
For questions or concerns, please email us at `privacy@cybertrader.game`.
